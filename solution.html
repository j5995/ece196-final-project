3D modeled enclosure that mounts to existing blinds and contains the stepper motor, PCB, and gearing.

  2. 2Output gear teeth: engages the chain loop on the user's existing blinds
  3. 3Ventilation slots: allows air to enter the enclosure and heat to escape
