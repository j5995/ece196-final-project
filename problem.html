Problem Definition

UC San Diego students living in dorms, bedrooms, and shared spaces frequently use manually operated chain loop blinds — left in one position all day. This causes monitor glare, excessive heat, or rooms too dark that rely on artificial lighting.

Problem Motivation

We've personally experienced the frustration of forgetting to adjust blinds. Poor lighting affects productivity, disrupts sleep patterns, and reduces overall quality of life — problems that a small piece of automation can completely eliminate.
