Problem Definition

People who are visually impaired face challenges in navigating and interacting with the real world.

Problem Motivation

We decided to work with this problem because of our appropriate skillsets and interest to tackle this problem. We thought this problem would be a good use case of basic hardware and software integration.

Existing Solutions

Meta Glasses by Meta

  • Strengths:
    • Advanced AR capabilities
    • Integration with Meta ecosystem
  • Weaknesses:
    • High cost
    • Limited accessibility features for visually impaired
    • Short battery life

NOA by biped.ai

  • Strengths:
    • Designed specifically for the visually impaired
    • Obstacle detection and navigation assistance
  • Weaknesses:
    • Potentially high price
    • Wearing a vest can reduce mobility
