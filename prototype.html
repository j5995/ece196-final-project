11 / Prototype

App and physical prototype.

AutoBlinds combines a mobile BLE control app with a physical motorized blind attachment. The app sends open, close, and stop commands, while the prototype translates those commands into real movement on a standard chain-loop blind.

Mobile App

BLE control interface.

The AutoBlinds app gives the user simple control over the blind motor. From the phone, the user can start opening, start closing, or stop the motor once the blinds reach the desired position.

Physical Prototype

Motorized blind attachment.

The physical prototype mounts next to the dorm blind chain and uses a motor-driven sprocket to move the chain. This setup demonstrates how AutoBlinds can automate existing manual blinds without replacing the entire window system.
