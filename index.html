— Problem

Manual blinds get set and forgotten.

Most dorms and bedrooms have chain loop blinds that stay in one position all day leading to monitor glare, room overheating, and over reliance on lamps.

— Solution

A small device that handles it.

An ESP32-driven module clips onto existing blinds and opens or closes them automatically based on light levels, schedules, or a tap from a mobile app.
